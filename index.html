What is Zakat ?

“Zakat is recognising the blessings and wealth that Allah has bestowed upon us. It is a religious obligation on every Muslim that
has reached puberty, is sane and possesses the minimum required wealth (Nisab). It involves giving a set amount of 2.5%
of one’s wealth to those in need. The believing men and believing women are allies of one another. They enjoin what
is right and forbid what is wrong and establish prayer and givezakah and obey Allah and His Messenger. Those
Allah will have mercy upon them. and Indeed, Allah is Exalted in Might and Wise.” -Quran (9:71)
